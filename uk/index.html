Home teaching material with animations for better understanding of the knowledge to the subject IB101 Introduction to Logic.

For animations of truth tables are used the numbers 0 and 1 because of space efficiency and clarity :
  • The number 0 represents false, F.
  • The number 1 represents true, T.
    • 1. Basic connections - contain the basic of logic. These include : propositional connectives NOT, AND, OR , implication and equivalence . Without understanding this knowledge is not possible to understand the related materials.

    2. Truth table - example of achievable truth table
  • tautology - a special case of the result of a truth table (they contain 1)
  • contradiction - a special case of the result of a truth table (they contain 0)
  • interpretation - separate chapter of the truth table ( by the user themselves selects the variables and program then generates only one row of the truth table )

    • 3. CNF, DNF - transfers from the table to the Conjunctive Normal Form (CNF) and Disjunctive Normal Form ( DNF )

    4. Resolutions:
  • Resolution-tree - initial resolution - excample of general resolution tree
  • Liner resolution - The first process of refinement resolution
  • LI resolution - continuing softening of the linear resolution with other rules
  • LD resolution - continuing softening of the LI resolution with other rules
  • SLD resolution- continuing softening of the LD resolution with other rules
