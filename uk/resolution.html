    Resolution is used to search for the truth of the whole logical formulas using logical disproof: □ .

  • All samples will have a single entry: taken from practice 6.1
    Disproof the following set of formulas:
    S = {(q ∧ r) ⇒ p, q ⇒ (r ∧ ¬ p), ¬ ( ¬ r ∨ ¬ ( r ⇒ p)), p ⇒ q}
    after the transfer to the sets:
    {p, ¬ q, ¬ r}, {r, ¬ q}, {¬ q, ¬ p}, {r}, {p, ¬ r}, {q, ¬ p}
